🚀 Quick Start

Start Trading in Minutes

QuantDesk is built for active perp traders. Sign in with your Solana wallet and follow this flow:

  1. Connect Wallet: Sign-in with Solana from the terminal landing page. Your Early Tester badge mints automatically and unlocks MIKEY AI points.
  2. Activate Rewards: Open the referral panel to grab your invite link. Every funded referral earns trading-fee rebates plus bonus MIKEY credits for both traders.
  3. Fund & Segment: Deposit USDC or SOL into your master account, then spin up sub-accounts to isolate strategies or desks.
  4. Spin Up MIKEY Intel: Spend badge points to run MIKEY smart-money scans, social sentiment sweeps, and funding outlooks before you size into a market.
  5. Execute the Trade: Use the command palette (`QM`, `ORDER`, `POSITIONS`) or the order ticket to enter bracketed long/shorts with real-time margin and funding previews.
  6. Share & Team Up: Drop alpha in the `CHAT`, ping teammates with `NOTE`, and explore multi-sig team account support coming soon for coordinated desks.

Example daily pipeline:

  • Kick off with `QM BTC-PERP` to review depth, funding, and whale prints.
  • Launch `FLOW` + `NEWS` to surface whale inflows and Solana ecosystem headlines, then tap `MIKEY` to benchmark sentiment.
  • Submit an `ORDER` with protective brackets, confirm exposure in `POSITIONS`, set an `ALERT` on funding flips, and broadcast the playbook in `CHAT` for coordinated trades.

QuantDesk bridges the pro trading desk experience with Solana speed—fast onboarding, built-in incentives, and intelligence that keeps you ahead of the tape.

⚙️ Platform Performance & Data Edge

Execution & Latency

  • API Response: Sub-150ms typical REST responses with WebSocket snapshots refreshing every second.
  • Order Lifecycle: Sign, serialize, and dispatch Solana transactions in a few hundred milliseconds end-to-end.
  • Risk Feedback: Margin, funding, and liquidation metrics recompute instantly on each position update.

Data Ingestion & Coverage

  • News & Social: Crypto newswires, Twitter sentiment, and DeFi protocol feeds processed in real time for MIKEY alerts.
  • Market Intelligence: Whale flow, on-chain wallets, and funding deltas captured through dedicated Solana scanners.
  • Oracles & Prices: Pyth price feeds with confidence intervals, plus supplemental exchange data for redundancy.

AI Routing & Tooling

  • MIKEY Graph: Context-aware routing taps news, sentiment, whale flow, and funding modules for concise desk briefings.
  • Expansion Hooks: Additional APIs (Arkham, Dune, CryptoQuant) staged for future intelligence layers.
  • Operator Controls: Badge-driven point system governs AI usage so every trader knows the costs and rewards.

🏗️ Architecture at a Glance

Trader-Facing Layers

  • Terminal & Command Palette: Keyboard-driven execution, risk dashboards, and collaboration tools.
  • Perp Markets: Solana PDAs manage collateral, positions, and liquidations with audit trails.
  • Data Fabric: MIKEY intelligence, news feeds, whale flow, and oracle prices blend into a single desk view.

Security & Reliability

  • Non-Custodial: Wallet signatures stay client-side; PDAs enforce limits without exposing keys.
  • Resilience: JIT liquidity, Pyth feeds, and multi-provider RPC keep execution steady.
  • Transparency: Admin monitoring, audit logs, and roadmap updates show how the desk evolves.
