Risk Disclosure

⚠️ IMPORTANT RISK WARNING

Trading cryptocurrencies and perpetual contracts involves substantial risk of loss and is not suitable for all investors. You should carefully consider whether trading is suitable for you in light of your circumstances, knowledge, and financial resources.

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

This Risk Disclosure outlines the significant risks associated with using QuantDesk for cryptocurrency trading. Please read this carefully before using our platform.

1. Market Risk HIGH

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and unpredictable:

  • Prices can fluctuate dramatically in short periods
  • Market movements can result in significant losses
  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
  • External factors (regulations, news, market sentiment) can cause sudden price changes

2. Leverage Risk HIGH

Perpetual contracts allow for leveraged trading, which amplifies both gains and losses:

  • Small price movements can result in large losses
  • You may lose more than your initial investment
  • Margin calls and liquidations can occur rapidly
  • Leverage increases the risk of total loss of capital

3. Liquidation Risk HIGH

Your positions may be automatically liquidated if:

  • Your margin falls below maintenance requirements
  • Market conditions deteriorate rapidly
  • Oracle price feeds show adverse movements
  • Network congestion delays your transactions

4. Smart Contract Risk MEDIUM

QuantDesk operates on smart contracts deployed on Solana blockchain:

  • Smart contracts may contain bugs or vulnerabilities
  • Code upgrades may introduce new risks
  • Third-party dependencies may fail
  • Despite audits, risks may still exist

5. Oracle Risk MEDIUM

Price feeds and data sources may be unreliable:

  • Oracle manipulation or failure
  • Stale or incorrect price data
  • Network connectivity issues
  • Data source outages

6. Technology Risk MEDIUM

Technical issues may affect platform operation:

  • Network congestion and high transaction fees
  • Platform downtime or maintenance
  • Browser or device compatibility issues
  • Internet connectivity problems

7. Regulatory Risk MEDIUM

Cryptocurrency regulations are evolving and may impact trading:

  • Changes in laws and regulations
  • Government restrictions or bans
  • Tax implications of trading activities
  • Compliance requirements

8. Counterparty Risk LOW

As a decentralized platform, QuantDesk minimizes counterparty risk:

  • Non-custodial - you control your private keys
  • Smart contracts execute trades automatically
  • No central authority controls your funds
  • Transparent and auditable operations

9. User Error Risk MEDIUM

User mistakes can result in losses:

  • Incorrect transaction parameters
  • Wrong wallet addresses
  • Insufficient gas fees
  • Misunderstanding of platform features

10. Risk Mitigation Measures

QuantDesk implements several risk mitigation measures:

  • Multi-layer Circuit Breaker: Automatic risk management
  • Advanced Keeper Authorization: Enhanced security protocols
  • Dynamic Oracle Protection: Staleness detection and prevention
  • 95/100 QA Score: Comprehensive security auditing
  • Real-time Monitoring: Continuous risk assessment

⚠️ RECOMMENDATIONS

  • Only trade with funds you can afford to lose completely
  • Start with small amounts to understand the platform
  • Never invest more than you can afford to lose
  • Educate yourself about cryptocurrency trading risks
  • Consider seeking professional financial advice
  • Monitor your positions regularly
  • Use appropriate risk management strategies

11. No Investment Advice

QuantDesk does not provide investment advice. All trading decisions are your own responsibility. The platform is a tool for executing trades based on your own analysis and risk tolerance.
