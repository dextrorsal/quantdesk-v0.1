Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how QuantDesk collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our decentralized trading platform.

1. Information We Collect

Public Blockchain Data

As a decentralized platform, QuantDesk operates on public blockchains. The following information is publicly visible:

  • Wallet addresses and transaction history
  • Trading positions and activities
  • Smart contract interactions

Platform Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance metrics
  • Error logs and debugging information

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our trading platform
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Develop new features and services

3. Data Storage and Security

Decentralized Architecture

QuantDesk operates on decentralized infrastructure:

  • Smart contracts are deployed on Solana blockchain
  • User funds are held in non-custodial wallets
  • No central database stores sensitive user data

Security Measures

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Multi-layer circuit breaker system
  • Advanced keeper authorization
  • Dynamic oracle staleness protection
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate with third-party services for enhanced functionality:

  • Oracle Providers: Pyth Network, Switchboard for price feeds
  • AI Services: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google for MIKEY-AI
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Wallet Control

You maintain full control over your assets:

  • Private keys are stored locally in your wallet
  • You can withdraw funds at any time
  • No centralized custody of user funds

Data Portability

As a decentralized platform, your data is inherently portable:

  • Transaction history is recorded on public blockchain
  • You can access your data through blockchain explorers
  • No vendor lock-in or data silos

6. Cookies and Tracking

We may use minimal cookies and tracking technologies for:

  • Essential platform functionality
  • Anonymous usage analytics
  • Security and fraud prevention

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

7. International Transfers

As a decentralized platform operating on global blockchain networks, data may be processed across international borders. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any data transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

QuantDesk is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our platform and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@quantdesk.app

  • Twitter: @QuantDeskApp

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your information while providing transparent, decentralized trading services.
